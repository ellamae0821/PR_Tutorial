Reading the Article

Hovering over the paper highlights an article. Clicking on an article will open the full article in the TEXT VIEW while double clicking an article zooms it in.

Accessing Links

Clicking on the article's page links shows the continuation page of the article as it is on the physical print. If the link is for an outside source, such as another website, it will open a new tab for the said link.

Changing Font Size

To change the font size, while on "Article View", click on the Increase Font / Decrease Font Logo to adjust the font.

Changing the Page

To go to the next page, click on the next page button found in the middle right of the page or back to go back to the previous page. You may also click on the next section button to jump to the next section.

Accessing Past Papers

To access previous issues, click on the Editions Icon at the right-side panel and select the by recent editions or access through searching archive date.

Print / Download / Share an article

To print an Article while on the "Article" mode, select an article on the navigation panel and find the {{PRINTER}} button on the top option. After clicking the print button, the printer set-up should pop-up.

Print / Download / Email a Screenshot

To print/download/email a portion of the page, you should be on "Browse" mode (full screen) of an article and click on the share (share button photo) button that will appear on the upper right. Crop the portion you wish to print/download and click print/download or email.

Listen to an Article

To listen to an article, while on "Article" view, click on an article you want to listen to and select the Listen to Article(Listen to Article img) button then a play button will be available for you click and start the audio.

Translate an Article

To translate articles, while on "Article" view, click on an article you want to translate and from the top menu select from the dropdown the Language you want it to be translated to.
